Glossary

TikTok Coins

Virtual currency used on TikTok for gifting creators during live streams.

Gifting

Sending virtual gifts during live streams as a way to support creators. Users purchase gifts with real money, which creators can exchange for cash.

Tap

An action consisting of tapping the screen to engage with a live stream, helping boost its visibility and reach.

Share

The act of sending a live stream to other users or platforms, either through direct messages, social media, or by copying the link. This helps increase the stream's reach and visibility to a broader audience.

LIVE Battle

A feature where two creators go live simultaneously and compete for gifts from viewers. It is shown through a split screen. The creator who receives the most gifts during the battle wins

Double

"x2" (times two) multiplier is a feature that temporarily doubles the points a creator earns from gifts during a specific period. When activated, any gifts sent by viewers contribute twice their usual point value to the creator's score. This multiplier is often used strategically to gain an advantage over opponents in live battles, as accumulating more points can enhance the creator's visibility and ranking on the platform

Triple

"x3" (times three) multiplier is a feature that temporarily triples the points a creator earns from gifts during a specific period. When activated, any gifts sent by viewers contribute three times their usual point value to the creator's score. This multiplier is often used strategically to gain an advantage over opponents in live battles, as accumulating more points can enhance the creator's visibility and ranking on the platform

LIVE Goal

A feature that allows creators to set a target for gifts or other forms of support during a live stream. Viewers can see the goal and contribute.
